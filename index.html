Expert Nephrology Care in Pharr, Texas

At Valley Kidney Center, we provide compassionate, patient‑focused nephrology care to families across the RGV. Our mission is to support long‑term kidney health through personalized treatment, education, and a commitment to improving quality of life for every patient we serve.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Savita Koolwal, MD

Dr. Koolwal is a board‑certified nephrologist and internal medicine physician with over 44 years of medical experience. She has dedicated her career to caring for patients with kidney disease, hypertension, electrolyte disorders, and complex chronic conditions.

A graduate of medical school in 1982, Dr. Koolwal has spent decades providing renal care to families throughout the Rio Grande Valley. Patients value her for her thorough evaluations, clear communication, and commitment to long‑term kidney health.

At Valley Kidney Center, Dr. Koolwal focuses on evidence‑based treatment, patient education, and individualized care plans designed to support each patient’s overall well‑being.
