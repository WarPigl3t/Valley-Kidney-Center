About Valley Kidney Center

At Valley Kidney Center, our story is rooted in compassion, community, and a deep commitment to caring for families across the Rio Grande Valley. For decades, we have welcomed patients with warmth, understanding, and a promise to walk alongside them through every step of their kidney health journey.

We believe that great medical care begins with listening — truly listening — to each person who walks through our doors. Every patient brings their own story, their own challenges, and their own hopes. Our role is to honor that story with respect, clarity, and thoughtful, individualized care.

Meet Dr. Savita Koolwal

Dr. Savita Koolwal is a board‑certified nephrologist and internal medicine physician with over 44 years of medical experience. Since beginning her career in 1982, she has dedicated her life to helping patients understand and manage kidney disease with confidence and compassion.

Patients often describe Dr. Koolwal as warm, patient, and deeply attentive. She takes the time to explain conditions clearly, answer questions thoroughly, and ensure that every patient feels supported and informed. Her approach is rooted in kindness and a genuine desire to improve the lives of those she serves.

For Dr. Koolwal, nephrology is more than a specialty — it is a calling to care for people during some of the most vulnerable moments of their lives. Her long‑standing commitment to the Rio Grande Valley reflects her belief in building lasting relationships and providing care that feels personal, steady, and trustworthy.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional kidney care that honors the dignity, well‑being, and unique needs of every patient. We strive to create an environment where individuals feel safe, understood, and empowered to take an active role in their health.

We believe in blending medical expertise with genuine human connection — because healing is not only about treatment, but also about trust, comfort, and compassion.

How We Care

At Valley Kidney Center, we approach care with warmth and intention. We take the time to understand your health, your concerns, and your goals. Our team works closely with each patient to create personalized treatment plans that support long‑term kidney health and overall well‑being.

Whether you are managing chronic kidney disease, navigating dialysis options, or seeking guidance for hypertension or electrolyte imbalances, we are here to support you with clarity, compassion, and steady medical expertise.

You are never just a chart or a diagnosis — you are a person, and your health matters deeply to us.
